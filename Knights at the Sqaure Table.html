Knights of the Square Table

Chess, like life, is an existential game. All living creatures including us as kings are in a continuous game for survival until we are checkmated and the game is over.
It’s a game of calculated risk and wit. Join us to battle it out with like-minded knights to challenge wit and some cash prizes.

Knights of the Square Table

Chess Competitons

The chess Tournament will be held on lichess app and will be of two days.



Rules


  1. Time format( for each match):- 5 minutes + 3 seconds increment
  2. Duration of tournament per day :- approx. 2 hours.
  3. Number of rounds :-
    DAY 1 - 7
    DAY 2 - 6
  4. On First day, all players will have a mixed match in swiss format(boys and girls together).
  5. Top 25 players (depend on number of entries) will be selected for rounds for second day. On second day, players will have to turn on their camera on google meet.
  6. Players need to have a lichess I'd in order to participate.
  7. All participants must upload the scan copy of id proof on the Google form.


Note:-
If any case of cheating is found, player will be reported and banned from lichess.
No claims of any result is allowed.
Final decision will be taken by organisers.
Pairing will be done by software itself.
