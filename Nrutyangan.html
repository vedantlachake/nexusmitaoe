BEST OUT OF WASTE

An open theme art event where the participants create art pieces out of scrap material like newspaper, paper dishes, tissues, balloons, old toys, cardboard etc which are no longer useful for their original purpose.



Rules


  1. The participants have creative freedom, unrestricted to any theme, to make anything they like out of their choice of materials
  2. They have to click pictures of their craft, minimum 3 (one of their craft, one of the participants with the craft, one with the name, registration ID and date of submission of participant, written on it) to maximum, clearly documenting all parts intricacies, in clear.
  3. Participants should also upload a short, 30 second clip of any part of the crafting process, as proof. This video need not be a time-lapse of the entire process, just some part of the process should be documented
  4. The submission should be in JPEG format with the original color of the art.
  5. The submission should follow the naming conventions. "Registration ID - name" .
  6. The size of the photographs should not exceed 10 MB.
  7. Participants must add a 1-3 line description about their creation in terms of usability, durability, creativity, innovation, etc while submitting.

ART BAZAAR

An Open Art Competition

ART Bazaar is an Open artwork competition where the participants can submit any of their Best Art work. There is no limit to the creativity here. ARTWORK CAN BE: Painting, sketching, doodle, mandala ART, doodling, papercutting, Rangoli etc.



Rules


  1. Artwork should be submitted in JPEG format.
  2. Photograph size should not exceed 10MB
  3. Edited photos, use of filters is strictly prohibited.
  4. Each participant has to write his/her registration id, name and date of submission on his artwork.
  5. Each participant has to upload two photographs, one of the main and a selfie with the art and a 30 second video while preparing the artwork.
  6. The submission should follow the naming conventions as "Registration ID - Name "
  7. Photo submitted should be clearly visible for judging.
  8. Art work provided should not have any glass frames

Note: The Video should be One Shot, No Transition. Register Now

HAND LETTERING


In this competition participants will be given a short phrase based on THEME, wherein they need to write it as creatively as then can. This can be done using various writing techniques, one of which is calligraphy. You can also use digital aids to do so. Beautiful writing with proper font size varying it wherever necessary will make it look more pleasing.
