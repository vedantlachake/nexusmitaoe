Sur Aur Taal

“Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination, and life to everything.” – Plato
For centuries, music has given voice to the imagination of scholars, artists, prisoners, war heroes and mundane workers alike. Let’s add a little fun and joy to the mix and rejoice in the basking warmth and strength of music.

Sur Aur Taal

My Bae Karaoke


You have to make a video singing with karaoke. Just try to give your best shot and upload the video on the link given below.



Allowed Participation: Solo and Duets

Rules


  1. The length of the video should be less than or equal to 5 minutes.
  2. You can use apps like Smule, Starmaker, etc. to record the video but don’t use voice filters. The use of any filter can lead to disqualification.
  3. The whole video should be a single shot. Merging multiple shots is not allowed.
  4. Shoot video in the bright room so that you are visible.
  5. There shouldn’t be any noise in your video. Try to shoot at the place where there is no noise or negligible noise.
  6. Don’t place the camera too close to your face. Make sure at least half of your body is visible in the video.
  7. Don’t do lip-syncing in the video. Recording audio and video separately and then merging them is not allowed.
  8. For duets also one can use apps like Smule, Starmaker, etc.

General Guidelines:

  • One person can submit multiple videos in one competition only if its type is different. Like one can submit his/her solo as well as a duet but not two solos.
  • Any changes in the type of performance (like one was performing duet but now wants to perform solo) must be told before submitting the video.
  • Failure in the submission of the video before the given deadline will lead to disqualification of the candidate and no refund will be given.
  • The final results will be based on 20% weightage to online votes and 80% weightage to scores by judges.
  • Result given by the committee will be the final result.
