Lit-Orate

“Speech is power: speech is to persuade, to convert, to compel.”
-Ralph Waldo Emerson

We understand the power of speech and want to give you a platform to speak freely and articulate your thoughts with flair and love and finesse. Be it carefully written poetry or spontaneous prose, well-documented thoughts or spur of the moment epiphanies, we have room for all.

QDE

Discussion


It is the combination of brainstorming, debate and group discussion which will challenge your reasoning and thinking abilities and provide opportunities to showcase and enhance your public speaking skills. In this competition a topic will be given and participants have to put forth their ideas, views and opinions. Participants have the good time to remark and comment on opinions placed by fellow participants.



Rules


  • Phase I: Introductory Speeches

    1. All participants are expected to deliver a 90 seconds long speech and rebuttal questions will be entertained after each speech.
    2. Number of questions asked would be at the discretion of the moderator.

  • Phase II: Moderated discussion

    1. The sub topic will be announced along with the number of speakers allowed and amount of time allotted for each speech.
    2. The moderator would ask for interested speakers and the participants would fill the slots by using the ‘Raise Hand’ feature.
    3. The participants would start giving their speeches in the order read out by the moderator. In case, more slots are available, the moderator would ask for interested participants and they’ll be filled.
    4. Questions to the speakers will be entertained at the end of a sub topic. Number of questions asked would be at the discretion of the moderator.
    5. The moderator would announce the next sub topic and so on, till all the topics have been exhausted.

  • Phase III: Concluding statements

    1. There are only seven slots in this phase and anyone who wishes to speak would need to send a request to the moderator before the competition along with a justification of a couple of line stating why you should be given the chance to speak in the conclusion.
    2. Each of the seven speakers would get 60 seconds each. No questions to the speakers would be entertained.

Lit-Orate

Poetry Slam


In this competition participants perform spoken word poetry before a live audience who decides which poem wins or loses. This competition is providing poetry enthusiasts to reveal their poetic ethos. The poem can be performed in any language.



Rules


  1. For online competition the participants have to record their performance and post on their instagram account and they need to tag MITAOE Literary Club.
  2. The poem should be recorded within 30 second.
  3. The video can have 1-2 paragraphs in 30 seconds.
  4. The voice and the picture should be clear. The participants need to ensure their is no background noise and the face and expressions should be clearly visible with ample amount of light.
  5. Winners will be decided on basis of likes, comments and shares for their performance
  6. The submission should follow the naming conventions as "Registration ID - Name "
  • Registration:

    1. After successful upload of the recorded performance on Instagram account the registration will be confirmed. (Do not forget to tag MITAOE Literary Club)
    2. No registration amount.
