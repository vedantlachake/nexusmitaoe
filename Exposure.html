Exposure

Photography is all about practicing to click pictures at correct settings at one go and patience to achieve it. A single photograph can have stories hidden inside it, emotions which speak for itself and memories which last forever. The time can never stop, but the captured moments in the photograph can pause the time for a while. Let’s freeze the time together to create beautiful memories.

Open Theme Photography Competition


A photography competition for everyone out there who has the rare factor to see the world with a different perspective. This competition will Showcase the world what a photographer sees with his lensed eye. The photos will be judged by panel of experts and organizing team. This competition is not only limited to college students but open to everyone out there so that everyone gets a chance to showcase their talent.


Theme:
Summer
Macro
Quarantine


Rules


  1. Maximum two entries per person
  2. Participant must provide the EXIF data
  3. The photographs should be original and no watermark of any kind should be on the photographs
  4. Size of the photograph must be more than 1 MB
  5. This competition is open to all you can participate irrespective of your age and profession.

Manipulation Challenge


A competition specially designed to challenge the Creativeness of a person. The participants will be provided with 8 different images and they’ll have to use all of the 8 files to create an Image. The image must comprise of all 8 images and nothing other than the provided assets can be used. The artist must be familiar with Adobe Photoshop or Lightroom as manipulations should be done using these softwares only. For participating in the same the participants must submit the the screen recording while they’re manipulating the images, without screen recording participants will be disqualified.



Rules


  1. Use the provided images only
  2. You can not add images and use every image provided.
  3. You can only use photoshop and lightroom.
  4. The artworks ratio must be 169 minimum resolution of 1920 X 1080
